PROOF OF RESULTS

Real Clients. Real Results.

Every engagement is measured by outcomes. Here's what happens when businesses trust Datamuri with their data strategy.

+312% Qualified Leads

Marketing Agency — Chicago, IL

Challenge: Agency was generating traffic but couldn't identify who was visiting their client sites or prioritize outreach effectively.

Solution: Deployed Visimuri across 14 client sites, enabling visitor identification and intent scoring. Built custom CRM integration to push high-priority contacts directly into their sales workflow.

Result: 312% increase in qualified lead volume over 90 days. Average response time dropped from 48 hours to 4 hours. ROAS increased by 2.1x.

62% Lower Customer Acquisition Cost

B2B SaaS Company — Austin, TX

Challenge: Significant ad budget waste targeting broad audiences with poor conversion rates and unclear attribution across channels.

Solution: Built a complete audience intelligence framework with firmographic segmentation, lookalike modeling, and multi-touch attribution tracking across all paid and organic channels.

Result: 62% reduction in customer acquisition cost. Campaign conversion rates improved by 3.4x. Attribution clarity revealed two underperforming channels and two underinvested winners.

+178% Revenue Growth (Y/Y)

E-Commerce Retailer — Jackson, MS

Challenge: Retailer had no visibility into which customer segments drove lifetime value or how to prioritize marketing spend across a growing product catalog.

Solution: Designed and deployed a custom data warehouse integrating Shopify, Google Ads, email platform, and offline POS data. Built segmentation models based on RFM (recency, frequency, monetary) analysis.

Result: Identified top 20% of customers driving 74% of revenue. Focused retention campaigns delivered 178% year-over-year growth with the same marketing budget.

41% Reduction in Churn

Professional Services Firm — Atlanta, GA

Challenge: High client churn with no early warning system or data-driven process for identifying at-risk accounts before they became losses.

Solution: Built a custom churn prediction model using engagement data, service utilization patterns, and communication frequency signals. Integrated alerts into the client's CRM with recommended intervention playbooks.

Result: 41% reduction in annual churn rate. Proactive interventions saved an estimated $2.1M in at-risk contracts within the first year of deployment.
